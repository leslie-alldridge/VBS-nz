History

Viking Brothers Strength and Conditioning was founded by the three Bakker Brothers (Ben, Henry and Nick) during 2016. After continued success in the field of powerlifting, they wanted to help others achieve their goals and become the strongest version of themselves.

After lots of blood, sweat and tears, Viking Brothers Strength and Conditioning was born on 51 Sydney St. Petone. Still relatively new, the gym boats an outstanding portfolio of specilist gym equipment and accessories that you won't find anywhere else.

Looking ahead, we will be hosting powerlifting competitions and offering personal training sessions for those looking to compete. As a registered UPANZ location, we'll be starting 2017 strong with many events.

Completion and Opening

Viking Brothers Strength and Conditioning has been open for over a year and hosted many competitions along the way. Our specialist equipment and passion for powerlifting has attracted nationwide talent to our gym. We have an eager group of members looking to progress themselves and master the big three lifts (bench press, deadlift, squat). In addition, we also cater for non-competitive lifters and those who are looking to get into shape - whether it be for weight loss, muscle gain or other motivations. Our experienced trainers are available throughout the day and are always happy to help.
